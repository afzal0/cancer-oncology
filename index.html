Overview

This data article presents a curated dataset of 185 integrative oncology and wellness services available across seven Australian metropolitan areas (Melbourne, Sydney, Perth, Darwin, Hobart, Brisbane, and Adelaide).

Value of the Data

  • Standardized Registry: Provides a single, standardized registry of publicly provided integrative oncology services across seven Australian cities, including fields for provider type, eligibility, and cost that are often missing from disparate listings.
  • Group-Program Focus: Supplies group-program identifiers (e.g., yoga, meditation, exercise, support groups) to facilitate reproducible service audits of evidence-informed modalities referenced in international guidance.
  • Verification Status: Documents verification status through verification notes, enabling transparent reuse and quality appraisal.

Data Structure

Each record includes the following standardized fields:

  • Name: Service name
  • Organization: Parent organization
  • Provider Type: Public hospital, NGO, university, council, etc.
  • Facility Type: Category of facility
  • Address, Suburb, Postcode: Geographic location
  • Phone, Website: Contact information
  • group_services_standardized: Controlled terms for group programs (yoga, meditation, exercise, support groups, etc.)
  • individual_services_standardized: One-on-one services (massage, acupuncture, psychology, etc.)
  • associated_services_standardized: Additional support services (social work, spiritual care)
  • Verification Notes: Quality assurance documentation (as of Oct 2025)
